  • What is PhishDetect

    PhishDetect is a tool designed to help users identify and avoid phishing websites by scanning URLs and analyzing them for signs of phishing.

  • How does PhishDetect work

    PhishDetect works by analyzing the provided URL for various characteristics common in phishing websites, such as unusual domain names, suspicious patterns, and known phishing indicators. It uses a combination of heuristic analysis, blacklist checking, and machine learning algorithms to determine the likelihood of a website being a phishing site.

  • What should I do if PhishDetect flags a website as phishing

    If PhishDetect flags a website as phishing, it is recommended to avoid entering any personal information on the site and to close the browser window. You can also report the site to your browser's phishing protection service and to other relevant authorities.

  • Can the service detect all phishing websites

    While our service is designed to detect a wide range of phishing attempts, it's possible that new or highly sophisticated phishing sites may evade detection. We continually update our algorithms to improve accuracy.

  • What is phishing and why should I be concerned

    Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers trick individuals into revealing sensitive information like passwords and credit card numbers by pretending to be a legitimate entity. It can lead to identity theft, financial loss, and other serious consequences.
